# CloudForge Hosting Business Plan

---

## Executive Summary

CloudForge Hosting is a cloud VPS service designed to provide affordable and easy-to-use virtual servers for students and startups. The platform focuses on simplicity, automation, and low-cost pricing compared to major providers.

Our service offers:
- VPS hosting with full root access
- Automated provisioning
- Integrated billing and support system
- Knowledge base and onboarding guidance

Target market:
- Students learning cloud computing
- Small startups
- Developers needing low-cost servers

Competitive advantage:
- Lower pricing than competitors
- Beginner-friendly setup
- Built-in support and documentation

## Market Analysis

### Market Trends
Cloud computing continues to grow rapidly as more businesses move online. VPS hosting is in high demand due to flexibility and scalability.

### Target Customers
- Students and learners
- Small businesses
- Freelance developers

### Competitor Analysis
Major competitors include:
- DigitalOcean
- Linode
- Vultr
- AWS Lightsail

These providers offer powerful services but are often more expensive and complex for beginners.

### Market Positioning
CloudForge positions itself as:
"Affordable and beginner-friendly VPS hosting"
