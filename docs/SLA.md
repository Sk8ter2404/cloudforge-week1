# Service Level Agreement

## Uptime Guarantee
- 99.5% monthly uptime guarantee
- Calculation: (Total time - Downtime) / Total time
- Excludes scheduled maintenance and external network issues (notified 24-48h in advance)

## Provisioning Time
- VMs provisioned within 3–7 minutes after payment confirmation
- Credentials delivered via email within 5–10 minutes

## Support Response Times
- Critical issues: 30 minutes response
- High priority: 2 hour response
- Normal priority: 12 hour response
- Low priority: 24–48 hour response

## Credits for Downtime
- 99.5% - 99.0%: 5% monthly credit
- 99.0% - 97.0%: 15% monthly credit
- Below 97.0%: 30% monthly credit

## Scheduled Maintenance
- Maximum 2–3 hours per month
- Performed during low-traffic hours (late night)
- 24–48 hour advance notice

## Service Reliability
- Continuous system monitoring
- Automatic restart for failed services
- Load balancing to maintain performance

## Security
- Encrypted connections and secure login
- Regular system updates and patches
- Optional backup services available

## Customer Responsibility
- Customers are responsible for managing their applications
- Misuse of resources may result in service suspension
